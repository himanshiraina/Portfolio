# how-it-works-section
With a "How it Works" section, you can show the steps to get a service or show your visitors how your product works.

## Tutorial
For detailed instruction's, view Solodev's [How to Create a "How it Works" Section](https://www.solodev.com/blog/web-design/how-to-create-a-how-it-works-section.stml)

## Demo
Try out a working example on [JSFiddle](https://jsfiddle.net/solodev/y9qdjpf2/).
